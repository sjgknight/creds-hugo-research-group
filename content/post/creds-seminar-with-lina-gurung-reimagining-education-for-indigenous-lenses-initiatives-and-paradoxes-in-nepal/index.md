Lina  Gurung is a Nepali scholar with Gurung heritage. Her PhD was on experiences  of online and distance education learners in Nepal, and she has worked as a  gender coordinator in a project enhancing access of teacher professional  development using ICT. Her interests in digital pedagogy, digital divides and  decolonial education will inform her talk exploring how to integrate and  sustain initiatives in education from Indigenous perspectives. Lina will  highlight paradoxes encountered in concrete work undertaken at Kathmandu  University School of Education.
